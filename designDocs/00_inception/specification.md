# project scope

**Project Title:**
AI Marketing Intelligence Agent
**Objective:**
Develop a web-based AI agent that analyzes a business idea, studies
competitors, collects their social media data, and generates data-driven
marketing strategies and content plans.
**Main Features:**
● Competitor discovery
● Social media scraping
● Engagement analytics
● AI-powered insights
● Automatic campaign recommendations
**Tech Stack:**
Next.js, Express.js, MongoDB, Playwright, OpenAI API
**Deliverables:**
● Admin dashboard
● Competitor analytics reports
● Automated campaign generator


Build an **AI Marketing Intelligence Agent** that:

1. Analyzes a business idea
2. Finds competitors
3. Collects their social media data
4. Extracts insights
5. Generates campaign suggestions
Not a fully autonomous decision support tool.
**System Architecture (simple & practical)
Step 1 — Input**
User enters:
● Project idea
● Industry
● Target country (ex: Tunisia)
● Keywords
**Step 2 — Market Research (AI + Web)**
Agent:


● Searches Google
● Finds competitors
● Classifies them (startup / leader / local / international)
Output:
● Competitor list
● Market summary
● SWOT style analysis
**Step 3 — Social Media Scraping**
For each competitor:
● Instagram/Linkedin/facebook followers
● Posting frequency
● Top posts
● Engagement rate
● Hashtags
● Content type (video / reel / static)
Tools:
● Apify / Playwright / Puppeteer


● Or official APIs
Output:
Table like:
| Brand | Followers | Avg Likes | Posts/week | Top format |
Engagement |
**Step 4 — AI Reverse Engineering (Insights)**
Use AI to:
● Detect patterns
● Best posting time
● Most used hashtags
● Content themes
● What performs best
Output:
● “Competitors using reels get 3x engagement”
● “Educational posts outperform promos”
● “Peak time: 7–9 PM”
**Step 5 — Campaign Generator**


AI generates:
● 30-day content calendar
● Post ideas
● Captions
● Hashtags
● Ad angles
● Hooks
Example:
Day 1 → Reel → Behind the scenes
Day 2 → Carousel → Tips
